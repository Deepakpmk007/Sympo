Triviaio

First, the team of three members, then one player of the team must sit with his/her back to the screen. The other two members are must describe the word without saying the word directly, making acting/sounds and using gestures.

    Rules:
  • Divide the players into teams of three. Each team will take turns being the active team and the guessing team.
  • One player from the active team sits with their back to a screen or a board where random images are displayed. This player is in the "hot seat" and will be guessing the word associated with the images.
  • The guessing team (the other two players) takes turns selecting an image from a deck or a digital library. They display the image to the rest of the active team, except for the player in the hot seat.
  • The two players who can see the image must describe it to the player in the hot seat without using the actual word associated with the image. They can use gestures, act out scenes related to the image, make sounds, or describe it in creative ways to convey its meaning.
  • The player in the hot seat tries to guess the word associated with the image based on the descriptions provided by their teammates. They have a limited time to guess, usually set by a timer..
  • Keep track of how many words the player in the hot seat guesses correctly within the given time limit. You can award points for each correct guess.
  • After a set number of rounds or when each player has had a chance to be in the hot seat, rotate roles, and let another team take their turn.
  • The team with the highest score at the end of the game wins.
